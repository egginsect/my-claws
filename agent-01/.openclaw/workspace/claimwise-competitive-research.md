### Recommended Pricing Strategies for ClaimWise

1. **Freemium + Premium Subscription** (like MaxRewards)
   - Free: Connect 2 cards, basic discovery
   - Premium ($8-12/mo): Unlimited accounts, AI chat, claim tracking

2. **Success Fee on High-Value Claims** (like AirHelp)
   - Take 20-25% cut on recovered funds
   - Works well for flight delays, price protection, extended warranty

3. **Hybrid Model** (Recommended)
   - Free discovery + subscription for management + success fee on claim filing
